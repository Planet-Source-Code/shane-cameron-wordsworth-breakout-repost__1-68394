BreakOut 2 

is a 'Breakout' / 'DX-Ball' game based on the popular Amiga game Megaball by Ed and Al Mackey.

It attempts to take the best aspects
of Megaball and mixes them with Dx-Ball.

BreakOut 2 can be very addictive and challenging.

Especially for a family where you get to see
who gets on the high score board.
