System Requirements

 

Minimum and Recommended System Requirements

  

Minimum System Configuration:

If your system does not meet any one of the following minimum system requirements, BreakOut 2 will not perform properly.

Microsoft® Windows® 95/98/Me/2000

Pentium™ II class processor, 333 MHz or faster

32MB RAM or greater

16-bit DirectX 7-compatible sound card

8MB or Higher / video card , 16-bit color depth, DirectX 7- compatible

DirectX 7 (included)

 

IMPORTANT!

In addition to meeting the Minimum System Requirements, it is highly recommended that you ensure that your computer's components are utilizing the most recent software drivers. 

 Recommended System Configuration:

The BreakOut2 Team recommends the following system specifications for your computer to run BreakOut 2 with the best possible performance.

 

Pentium™ III class processor, 450 MHz or faster

64MB RAM or Greater
